The Architecture of Rest

The Solis Lounge Chair was conceived through a rigorous study of human ergonomics and Japanese minimalist principles. We stripped away the superfluous to find the essence of comfort.

The joinery is a testament to heirloom quality, utilizing traditional mortise and tenon techniques that allow the wood to breathe and move over decades of use.
